The Story of the Global Food System

Every issue has a story.

This short animated film provides an overview of the story of the global food system. From the wheat plains of Australia to the trading floors of the Chicago Board of Exchange, the film narrates the many layers of the structures in place that bring food to consumers on a day-to-day basis and reveals the inequalities and imbalances that such a food system creates in societies across the globe. Watch, learn, and share!

Types of Corporations

Info

Most agribusiness corporations operate across world borders in many countries. Its operations are diverse and involve extensive capital and infrastructure to maintain its business and growth. Oftentimes, these corporations can control markets and prices, thus giving them immense power within the food system. This section focuses on those corporations that are involved in the global food system and visually explains its structure and role in this system.
